Tips for the visitors

  1. Do not bring food or liquid into a gallery and do not chew gum.
  2. Touch only the pieces designated for touching, such as interactive displays and buttons that turn on audio or video.
  3. Be selective and go at a pace that's comfortable for you. Some visitors will want to see lots at a fast pace; others will want to slowly admire a small number of artifacts. Both are correct!
