HORSE

Horses once roamed free over grasslands in North America, South America, Europe, Asia, and Africa.

  • Scientifc name: Equus caballus
  • Average height: 1.6 - 1.7m
  • Mass: 420kg
  • Lifespan: 5-6 years

Horses are obligate nasal breathers. This means that they are unable to breathe through their mouth as humans can. Horses can only breathe through their nose
